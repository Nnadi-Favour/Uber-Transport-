Privacy Policy

Information We Collect:

Personal Information: When you register with Uber Transport, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details.
Location Information: We may collect your location information when you use our services, including GPS data.
Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our services, including device type, operating system, and browser type.
Usage Data: We collect data about how you interact with our services, including the pages you visit and the actions you take.

How We Use Your Information:

Providing Services: We use your information to provide and improve our services, including processing ride bookings and payments.
Customer Support: We may use your information to respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
Personalization: We use your information to personalize your experience and deliver relevant content and recommendations.
Security: We use your information to protect our services and prevent fraudulent activities.

Data Sharing:

With Drivers: We share your information with drivers to facilitate ride bookings and ensure a smooth experience.
With Third Parties: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our services.
Legal Compliance: We may share your information when required by law or to protect our rights and interests.

Data Security:

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices:

You can access and update your personal information by logging into your Uber Transport account.
You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails or notifications from us.

Policy Updates:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
